Why the Network Changes Everything

Any professor can assign an AI exercise. Any student can use ChatGPT. The AI tools are free and getting better every month. That’s not a competitive advantage for anyone.

The competitive advantage is what happens after the student does the work — who sees it, who evaluates it, and who the student becomes connected to as a result.

Every product on this platform is built around a simple idea: students learn more when a working professional evaluates their work than when a rubric does. Faculty teach better when they know what industry actually needs. And universities differentiate when their graduates leave with both a credential and a network.

ZAI’s proprietary technology handles the matching, preparation, and delivery. But the value isn’t the technology. The value is the human on the other end of every experience.
