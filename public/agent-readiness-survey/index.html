The Agent Readiness Gap — 2026 Survey

The conversation about AI is shifting. A year ago, organizations asked how their people would use AI. Today, the question is what work AI agents will do on their own.

This 4-minute survey explores how ready organizations actually are. Your responses will be aggregated into a research report published by ZAI Institute later this year. No names, companies, or identifying details will be shared.

9 questions  ◊  ~4 minutes  ◊  Anonymous
Question 1

Which best describes your current role?

Question 2

Which best describes your industry?

Question 3

Which best describes your organization's current use of AI agents?

By "agents" we mean software that completes work autonomously, not just chatbots that answer questions.

Question 4

Where do you see the strongest near-term opportunity for AI agents in your organization?

Choose up to three.

Question 5

How would you describe your organization's policy for human oversight of AI agents?

Question 6

When an AI agent makes a meaningful decision in your organization, who is accountable for the outcome?

Question 7

How would you rate your trust in the output of a well-deployed AI agent compared to the output of a competent human employee doing the same work today?

Question 8

Two years from now, what percentage of your organization's total work do you expect will be performed primarily by AI agents rather than humans?

Question 9

What is the single biggest barrier to deploying AI agents in your organization right now?

Question 10 (Optional)

What else should leaders understand about preparing for an agent-driven workforce?

Anything you want to share. Will not be attributed.
